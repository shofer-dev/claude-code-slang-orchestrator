Honest framing. slang and native dynamic workflows both reach real, working implementations at ~0 coordination cost — the difference is what's guaranteed: slang's structure is analyzable before it runs, tool-scoped and contract-checked while it runs, and rendered as diagrams after — versus LLM-authored JS you have to trust. Turn-by-turn LLM coordination additionally risks drift (a measured batch showed silent false-convergence — reports success, ships nothing). Reach for slang when you want guarantees & auditability, not "the model coordinated some subagents this time."
